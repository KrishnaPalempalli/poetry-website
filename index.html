Poetry Day With Mixed Emotions!
I am presenting to you all a variety of poems (silly & sad ones)!
Limerick #1: "Old Man Bill"
There is a very Old Man named Bill,
Who always goes out and likes to chill.
Once, he felt a cold breeze,
This caused Old Bill to freeze.
And now, for cold he has to take a pill.
Limerick #2: A French friend
My funny and fancy friend came from France.
He was singing and trying to dance.
But, he fell on the floor.
His yell was a loud roar.
Now medication costs him some finance.
Limerick #3: Lazy Sister
My elder cousin is very lazy.
She ate chips crunch, champ, crunch like crazy,
She drank a lot of tea,
While watching the TV.
She had to pee although she was dozy.
The Villanelle: “The Loss”
I can never forget that dark and doomy day.
That incident will always remain.
The day my most lovable pet passed away.
I cried for hours with dismay.
And then the sky started to rumble and rain.
I can never forget that dark and doomy day.
Now, I have no one to play.
Memories haunt, as ghosts of pain.
The day my most lovable pet passed away.
Overcoming this situation was a delay.
We decided not to have a pet again and I felt like a lonely and lost train.
I can never forget that dark and doomy day.
Eventually, I realized death is the final way.
So, live every moment and do not have a constrain.
The day my most lovable pet passed away.
In this world, no one will have a permanent stay.
I felt as if my love and emotion was water flowing down the drain.
I can never forget that dark and doomy day.
The day my most lovable pet passed away.
